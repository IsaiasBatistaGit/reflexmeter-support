Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Reflex Meter?

Simply tap the red dots as they appear on screen! Try to tap as many as possible within the 60-second time limit. Your goal is to achieve the highest score and fastest reaction times.

What do the skill levels mean?

The app tracks your average reaction time and assigns you a skill level based on your performance. From "Neural Ninja" for ultra-fast reflexes to "Slow Start" for those still warming up!

Does the app collect my data?

No! Reflex Meter operates entirely on your device and doesn't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.

How can I improve my score?

Practice makes perfect! Focus on the dots as soon as they appear, and try to maintain a consistent rhythm. The more you play, the better your reflexes will become.

Is the app suitable for children?

Yes! Reflex Meter is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. It's a great way to improve hand-eye coordination and reaction times.
